Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I verify my TEVTA student status?

During signup, enter your TEVTA roll number and institute. Our system will verify your enrollment with TEVTA records within 24-48 hours.

Is the platform free for students?

Yes! WorkNStay AI is completely free for TEVTA students. We charge a small commission from employers and hostel owners only.

How does the AI matching work?

Our AI analyzes your skills, education, and preferences to match you with jobs and hostels that best fit your profile. The higher the match score, the better the fit.

Are the listed hostels verified?

All hostels go through our verification process including physical inspection, owner verification, and ongoing review monitoring to ensure student safety.
