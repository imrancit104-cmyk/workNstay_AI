Our Story

WorkNStay AI was born from a simple observation: TEVTA graduates, despite their valuable technical skills, often struggle to find suitable employment and safe accommodations near their institutes.

Founded by a team of passionate technologists and education advocates, we set out to create a platform that leverages artificial intelligence to match students with opportunities that truly fit their profiles.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of students across Punjab, connecting them with verified employers and safe, affordable hostels - all through a platform designed specifically for their needs.
